# Ancient World Maths

If we look at Babylonian and Egyptian systems, we can see early forms of model simplification.

In product work this reappears as:\n
- reducing a complex system to measurable proxies
- iterating from approximation to precision

Inline formula example: $LTV = ARPU \times lifetime$.

Block formula example:

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i
$$
